## Our Three Offerings

### (1) The Orchestration System — Flagship
A highly automated, multi-layered, deployment-agnostic, agentic AI orchestration system. Built by software architects. **Not vibe coding.** Deterministic, enterprise-grade results.

Best for:
- PoC and MVP building (€10K–€25K, days)
- Legacy system refactoring (reverse engineer → modern spec → implement)
- Enterprise custom product development (€50K–€200K, weeks)

Key properties: autonomous, orchestrated, deterministic, tech-stack agnostic, deployment agnostic.

### (2) Workflow Automation Framework
n8n-based automation for companies struggling with Monday.com limitations or broader ERP/CRM workflow pain. Frontend + API integration approach. Lighter-touch, faster to sell.

### (3) AI Training
Agentic AI development training. Door-opener. Growing need as the whole IT industry is in upheaval. Can be standalone or bundled with delivery projects.

## Competitive Positioning

**Our sweet spot:** AI enthusiasm + decades of industry experience. Neither the slow large agency nor the thin-on-experience solopreneur.

| Competitor Type | Weakness | Our Advantage |
