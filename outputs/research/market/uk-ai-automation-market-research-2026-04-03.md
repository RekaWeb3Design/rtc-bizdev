## Part 4: Competitive Positioning Analysis

### 4.1 Where Roll the Code Fits

The UK AI delivery market has a structural gap between two extremes:

**Left flank - Large Consultancies (Accenture, Deloitte, KPMG Digital, PA Consulting):**
- Deep enterprise credibility and established client relationships
- Slow (months to years), expensive (500K+ GBP), AI capability often bolted-on rather than native
- Not accessible to mid-market or scaleups
- Losing ground as clients demand faster, AI-native delivery

**Right flank - Solopreneurs, no-code builders, small automation shops:**
- Fast and cheap but lack enterprise experience, compliance awareness, and delivery consistency
- Cannot handle complex legacy systems or regulated environments
- No senior architecture oversight

**Roll the Code sweet spot:** The underserved middle - companies that need real software delivered fast, by people who understand enterprise context, at a price point that does not require a major procurement process. The Vapiano of Product Delivery: quality, affordable, scalable.
