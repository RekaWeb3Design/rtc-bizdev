Sizing Methodology

  • TAM ($550B): Total global custom software development and IT services market addressable by AI-augmented delivery models
  • SAM ($12B): AI-augmented software delivery in our target geographies (UK, Nordics, CEE). Calculated as: UK+Nordic IT services market ($120B) × AI-augmented delivery adoption (15%) × mid-market/enterprise share (65%)
  • SOM ($8M): Realistic 3-year capture. Based on 50–70 clients × €75K–200K ACV, through direct sales + 3 partner channels. Conservative <0.1% of SAM.

  • Enterprise AI budgets growing 38% YoY — accelerating demand for AI-native delivery partners
  • Talent shortage makes AI-orchestrated delivery increasingly attractive vs. traditional hiring
  • Regulatory pressure (EU AI Act) favours architect-supervised, deterministic systems over black-box automation
