## The Problem You're Living With

Construction project managers are spending **2 full days every week** assembling progress reports. They pull data from 5 different systems — project scheduling, cost tracking, site logs, subcontractor portals, document management — paste it into Excel, format it, and email it to clients. Multiply that across every active project, and your most experienced people are spending 40% of their week on admin instead of managing delivery.

**That's not a reporting process. That's a tax on your best people.**

## What We Build For Construction Operations Teams

We deliver **automated reporting workflows in days, not months** — connecting your existing systems into a single, hands-off reporting pipeline.

- **Automated data consolidation** — pull schedule progress, cost status, RFIs, and site updates from all 5 systems into one unified view, on a schedule you set
- **Client-ready reports generated automatically** — formatted, branded PDF or dashboard sent to each client weekly — no manual Excel work, no copy-paste errors
- **Exception alerts, not status meetings** — PMs get notified only when something is off-track (budget variance, schedule slip, overdue RFIs) so they can act instead of report
- **Plugs into what you already use** — works with Procore, Asta Powerproject, Aconex, SharePoint, Sage, or whatever combination your teams run
